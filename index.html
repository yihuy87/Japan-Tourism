Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, offers a perfect blend of traditional and modern attractions. Explore the bustling Shibuya, historic Asakusa, and the serene Meiji Shrine.

Kyoto

Known for its classical Buddhist temples, Kyoto is a city of rich history and culture. Don’t miss the iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.

Osaka

Osaka is a vibrant city known for its modern architecture, nightlife, and delicious street food. Visit the Osaka Castle and the lively Dotonbori district.

Hokkaido

Hokkaido is famous for its stunning natural landscapes, including hot springs, volcanoes, and ski resorts. Don’t miss the Sapporo Snow Festival in winter.
